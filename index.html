Who we are

A permanent home for niche software businesses — not a fund waiting to flip them.

Most acquirers of vertical software operate on a four-to-six year clock. We don't. Waverock is an operating holding company structured to hold and grow the businesses we acquire for the long term — which changes almost every decision about how we run them.

We buy great businesses in markets that outsiders often overlook. We leave founders in control of product, team, and customers. We handle the back office so they don't have to. And we ask every founder to roll 20% of their proceeds into Waverock equity, so we grow together.

Waverock is founder-led, AI-forward, and built in Scottsdale for the next generation of vertical software founders who want a real home for what they've built.

Why Waverock

Four things founders tell us we get right.

We are operators first. Every one of these pillars comes from what founders told us they wished a buyer would actually do — and what most buyers don't.

01

Founder-led, not fund-led

Our CEO has sat in your chair. Mike ran Attainia before Waverock — acquired, grew, and sold it to TA Associates. We know what it takes, and what it feels like.

02

Operational independence

You keep control of product, team, strategy, and vision. We align on a handful of growth and profitability guardrails, then get out of the way.

03

Shared equity, shared upside

Founders roll 20% of proceeds into Waverock equity. Management teams get equity at the Waverock level. When the platform wins, you win.

04

Blueprints, not playbooks

No 100-day cost-cutting plan. No vanity metrics. Just a shared services backbone — finance, HR, IT, compliance, AI — plus proven patterns you can adopt at your own pace.

The Waverock Way

We are a founder-led organization dedicated to building great software businesses. We get to know your business intimately, align on what great looks like, then give you the independence to get there at a pace that works for your team.

Mike Rozenfeld, CEO & Founder

What We Do DO

  • Preserve the culture and legacy you've built
  • Focus on sustainable, profitable growth
  • Make decisions from data, not anecdotes
  • Treat founder and employee equity as sacred
  • Practice gradual, positive change
  • Recruit, retain, and develop top talent
  • Share best practices across operating companies

What We Don't Do DON'T

  • Quick flips or financial engineering
  • 100-day radical cost-cutting playbooks
  • Dictate your market, vision, or team
  • Milk companies for short-term cash
  • Overburden businesses with debt
  • Chase vanity metrics or growth-at-all-costs
  • Impose unilateral unrealistic goals
Our Process

How a partnership comes together — typically in 90 days.

We run a diligent, respectful, and efficient process. No surprises, no drawn-out stalling. You'll know where we stand at every step.

01

Listen & understand

We start with a private conversation. No NDA needed for the first call. We want to understand your business, your goals, your team, and what a good outcome looks like for you.

02

Alignment & IOI

If there's a fit, we sign an NDA, share a data request, and within weeks submit an Indication of Interest outlining valuation, structure, and operating principles.

03

Diligence & planning

Focused, AI-accelerated diligence across finance, customers, tech, and team. Closing within 90 days of signed LOI. Deal expenses capped at 3% of EV.

04

Growth partnership

Your team stays in control. Shared services turn on as you want them. Equity rolls forward. Compounding begins — for the business, for your team, and for you.

Who we look for

The businesses that thrive inside Waverock share a few traits.

We prefer to be transparent about fit. If this sounds like your company, we'd like to hear from you — even if you're just starting to think about what's next.

Recurring revenue

SaaS or licensing models, $2–15M in ARR, with 90%+ net dollar retention and multi-year contracts.

Defensible niche

"Must-have" software for a specific vertical, with real switching costs and a strong position in your corner of the market.

Healthy economics

80%+ gross margins, EBITDA >$0.5M, consistent cash flow, low customer concentration, low cycle exposure.

North America

US and Canadian companies, with a bias toward founders who built close to their customers and loyal to their teams — not chasing the next venture round.

Committed team

Experienced management that wants to keep building. Founders who want either a graceful transition or a long runway alongside us.

Aligned partnership

Comfort rolling 20% of proceeds into Waverock equity and benefiting from our collective growth.
